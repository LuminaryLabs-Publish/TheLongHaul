## Main finding

`updateWorldStreaming()` reconstructs and republishes near-world preparation state every driving frame. Only Core World focus/update/reconciliation is gated by a cell-key transition. With `ACTIVE_RADIUS = 1`, the product caller creates at least 56 source-visible objects or arrays per driving frame before controller-owned work.

The controller additionally recreates focus state, desired/prefetch sets, eviction arrays, statistics, and pump results. The product also layers a manual three-cell prefetch strip over the controller's built-in `prefetchDistance: 3` policy without one canonical prefetch-plan result.
