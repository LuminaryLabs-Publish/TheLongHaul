Find the right yard

Five distinct freight roads lead to five believable depots. Only one has your receiving order. Wrong gates add twenty seconds to your golf-style score.

DriveW or ↑ accelerates. S or ↓ brakes then reverses. A and D steer. Shift boosts at extra fuel cost.
DeliverEnter a depot gate. Stop fully. Press E. Decoys keep the freight and mark themselves on the map.
NavigateM opens the paper map. It shows only roads and yards you have explored and never reveals the correct unchecked yard.
Protect the loadBrake for wildlife. Stay near the road. Save the single roadside recovery for a real emergency.
ScoreLower adjusted time wins. Wrong yards impacts lost cargo recovery and excess distance count against par.
CameraC switches between a readable chase camera and a low hood camera. Escape pauses. R retries the same course.
