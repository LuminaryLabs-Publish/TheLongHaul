Driver's briefing

Find the right yard

Five roads lead to five believable freight depots. Only one has your receiving order. Every wrong gate costs twenty seconds, so drive hard and read each road.

DriveW or ↑ accelerates. S or ↓ brakes, then reverses. A and D steer. Shift gives a fuel-hungry push.
DeliverEnter a depot's gate, stop the truck, and press E. A wrong yard keeps the cargo and the clock moving.
NavigatePress M for the paper map. It records only roads and depots you have explored. The correct yard is never marked early.
Protect the loadStay on the road, brake for wildlife, and save your single roadside recovery for a true emergency.
ScoreLower adjusted time wins. Wrong yards, impacts, lost cargo, recovery, and excess distance all count against par.
ControlsC changes camera. Escape pauses. E calls recovery when stranded. R restarts the same course.
